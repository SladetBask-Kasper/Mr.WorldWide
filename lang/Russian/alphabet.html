Russian Alphabet (Русский алфавит - Russkij alfavit)

Is it a difficult script to learn?

The Russian alphabet is really simple to learn. It is based upon the Greek alphabet just like our (roman) alphabet. So there are a ton of characters that are the same across all three alphabets. So both the Greek and Cyrillic alphabets are some of the easiest you can learn if you know the modern roman one (which I assume you do since you're currently reading text in that script).

The alphabet

On some letters I have given multible sounds, and with the exception of "O" they all basically have one sounds. The multible options given are to show how you could transliterate in diffrent ways but they all represent the same sounds.

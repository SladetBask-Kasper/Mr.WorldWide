Armenian Alphabet (Հայոց այբուբեն - Hayots’ aybuben)

Is it a difficult script to learn?

The Armenian alphabet is quite easy to learn since it is based upon the Greek alphabet which means that if you know any of Armanias sister systems then you'll find many furmiliar characters. The sister writing systems are: Greek, Latin (the one you're reading right now), Coptic, Georgian & Cyrillic.

What does "Aybuben" mean?

So "Aybuben" is just the name of the first two letters of the alphabet. Just like our word in english "Alphabet" is just the name of the first to letters of the Greek alphabet (which the roman one is based upon): Alpha Beta.

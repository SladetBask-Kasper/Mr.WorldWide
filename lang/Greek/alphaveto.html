Greek Alphabet (Ελληνικό αλφάβητο - Ellenikó Alpháveto)

Is it a difficult script to learn?

The Greek alphabet is really easy to learn. The roman alphabet (the one you're reading right now) is based upon the Greek alphabet, so you'll find many characters that are the same or similar across both alphabets. In fact, the English word "alphabet" comes from the first to letters of the Greek one (Alpha + beta = alphabet).

Two letter vowels & consonants

Letters Transliteration Sound
ΓΓ/γγ gg Hard G
ΓΚ/γκ gk Hard G
ΜΠ μπ mp b sound
ΝΤ ντ nt D sound
ΑΙ αι ai E sound *
ΕΙ ει ei i sound
ΟΙ οι oi i sound
ΥΙ υι yi i sound
ΟΥ ου oy U sound, like in Loot
ΑΥ αυ ay either av or af
ΕΥ ευ ey either ev or ef

* αι is either e or like the English word "yeah", so in the word "είναι" is pronounced "ine", but "και" is pronounced "kyeah", or like "kje". So it's pronounced either like a latin "e" or like a cyrillic "е".
